FAQ

What is a set? What is a rep?

A set is a group of reps. A rep is the number of times you lift and lower a weight in one set.

What is E-Lookup?

E-Lookup allows you to search for a specific piece of equipment or exercise a specific body part.

Who are F.I.T.?

F.I.T. was designed by Ursinus College students for an HCI class. They just wanted an easier way to workout and thought they should share their solution.
